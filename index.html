Where I Found Home

July 2026

It all started when I randomly watched your video. I instantly fell in love with the warmth of your smiles. I just wanted to know your names. Now it's all history. They say everything has a time, and you came perfectly on time to save me. Suddenly, I was not alone crying in the room. You were there through your music and words. I used to hate myself and was sad. You taught me how to love myself, slowly and gradually. You taught me how to smile. For you, I'd go every mile. Back home, I never thought I would get the opportunity to see you in person, that you would be closer to me than the screen, that I would see you smile in real life. Thank you for giving me the strength to never give up. Your music, your journey, and your words all give me encouragement through and through, telling me to keep trying, no matter what. No matter how much I thank you, it will never be enough.

Genuinely I Don't Know

July 2026

I sit here and wait for them to ask, Hoping they could discover the mask. Maybe someone could finally see That I just pretend to be full of glee. I was bursting with joy since the year began, Wondering how my July would pan. Now that the most important event of my life is over, Depression has taken over. I always make sure to check up on my friends, Ensuring no loose ends. But when it's my turn, why would no one ask me If I feel free? I understand everyone has difficult days; It is a spiraling maze. But it just takes a text To see if I'm perplexed. I have always been alone, Never letting the loneliness be known. I know this feeling won't always stay; I'll find my way, just not today.
